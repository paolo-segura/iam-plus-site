Where should I start?

Discovery — it's the entry point to the whole journey, and where everyone begins. From there the path opens to Breakthrough and Abundance, climbed in order, at your own pace. Our team will help you find the right next step for where you are.

Where are the programs held?

Discovery runs at House of Transformation (Ortigas, Pasig City) and House of Prime (Bacoor, Cavite) — and is now expanding to Davao, Cebu, and Bacolod. Graduates come from across the Philippines and beyond.

How long is each one?

Discovery is 2 full days, Breakthrough is a 3-day intensive, and Abundance is a multi-weekend journey of three Deep Dives across two months.

Is Abundance only for graduates?

Yes. Abundance is the pinnacle of the journey, reserved for graduates of Breakthrough — so everyone in the room arrives with the same foundation.

How do I pay?

Discovery and Breakthrough use secure online checkout (GCash, Maya, or card) on each program page — your seat is confirmed instantly. Abundance is by application: you apply and our team reaches out personally.
