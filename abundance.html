Do I need Breakthrough first?

Yes. Abundance is reserved for graduates of Breakthrough (which itself requires Discovery). It's the highest level of the journey, so you arrive with the foundation already in place.

Is Abundance just three weekends?

No — it's a full two-month coaching program. The core is ongoing: daily coaching calls, a weekly mastermind, and the Success Club keep you moving the whole way through. The three in-person Deep Dives are the milestones spaced across those two months (about 60–72 days), so the shifts have time to take root in your real life between them.

What's the theme this batch?

Each Abundance batch gets its own world. This one is Abundance 12 — The Game of My Life: where legends are made.

What's included in the investment?

All three Deep Dives, every exercise and material, the full A12 experience, a certificate of completion, and the I AM+ graduate community.

What's the refund / transfer policy?

Seats are transferable but non-refundable. If you can't make your batch, you can pass your seat to someone else or move to a later batch — just message us and we'll help you arrange it.

How do I reserve a seat?

Secure online checkout via GCash, Maya, or card. Your seat is confirmed instantly, with details sent to your email.
