Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026 I AM+ Coaching & Training Corp.

This Privacy Policy explains how I AM+ Coaching & Training Corp. collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you visit our website, register for a program, or get in touch with us. We handle your data in line with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173).

1 Information We Collect

  1. Details you provide when you register, book, or pay for a program — your name, email address, and mobile number.
  2. Information you submit through our inquiry forms (corporate, consultation, and staffing) and the Abundance application.
  3. Payment details, which are collected and processed by our secure payment provider — we do not store your card details.
  4. Basic technical information (such as browser and device data) that helps keep the website working and secure.

2 How We Use Your Information

  1. To process your enrollment and payment, and to issue your event ticket and QR code.
  2. To send booking confirmations, receipts, and event reminders by email and SMS.
  3. To respond to your inquiries and provide the programs and services you request.
  4. To improve our programs, website, and overall customer experience.

3 Email and SMS Communications

  1. The messages we send are transactional — confirmations, receipts, and reminders related to your booking.
  2. You may opt out of SMS at any time by replying STOP.
  3. We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

4 How We Share Your Information

  1. With trusted service providers who help us run the platform — our payment processor, hosting and database provider, and email and SMS providers — only as needed to deliver our services, and under confidentiality.
  2. When required by law, regulation, or a valid legal request.
  3. We never sell your personal information.

5 Data Storage and Security

  1. Your information is stored on secure, access-controlled systems.
  2. We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect it.
  3. Payments are handled by a PCI-DSS-compliant provider; we do not store your full card details.

6 Data Retention

  1. We keep your information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described here, or as required by law.

7 Your Rights

  1. Under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173), you have the right to access, correct, object to, or request deletion of your personal information.
  2. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8 Cookies

  1. We use only the cookies needed for the website to function and to understand basic usage. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

9 Changes to This Policy

  1. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be posted on this page with its effective date.

Questions about your privacy or your personal data? We're here to help. Reach out and we'll walk you through anything.
