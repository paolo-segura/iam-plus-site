Who is Coaching Mastery for?

HR leaders, executives, and managers — the people who spend their days developing everyone else. And yes: individual contributors who lead without the title are welcome too. If you carry other people's growth, this room is for you.

Is this a certification?

No. This is a transformation, a set of working tools, and a written 90-day roadmap you take home — plus a certificate of participation. It's built to change how you lead, not just add a line to your CV.
