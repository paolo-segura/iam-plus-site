Neither. Discovery is experiential — you'll do guided exercises that create real shifts, not just take notes from the back of the room.

No. Discovery meets you where you are — whether you're just starting out or already running a business.

What's included in the fee?+

The full 2-day program, all exercises and materials, a certificate of completion, and access to the I AM+ graduate community.

Seats are transferable but non-refundable. If you can't make your batch, you can pass your seat to someone else or move to a later batch — just message us and we'll help you arrange it.

How do I pay?+

Secure online checkout via GCash, Maya, or card — your seat is confirmed instantly with a ticket and QR code sent to your email.
