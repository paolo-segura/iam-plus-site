Who is Breakthrough for?

Leaders, founders, and high performers ready to remove what's been capping them — and step into the next level of how they lead and perform. Not sure it's your moment? Talk to our team and we'll help you decide.

How intense is it, really?

Intense. Breakthrough is designed to take you to the edges you normally avoid — inside a held, safe space with trained facilitators. You'll be stretched, challenged, and changed — and you'll lead differently because of it.

Is it really three full days?

Yes — three consecutive full days. Block them off completely. The work compounds across the three days; missing one breaks the arc.

What's included in the fee?

The full 3-day intensive, all exercises and materials, a certificate of completion, and access to the I AM+ graduate community.

What's your refund / transfer policy?

Seats are transferable but non-refundable. If you can't make your batch, you can pass your seat to someone else or move to a later batch — just message us and we'll help you arrange it.

How do I pay?

Secure online checkout via GCash, Maya, or card — your seat is confirmed instantly, with a ticket and QR code sent to your email.
